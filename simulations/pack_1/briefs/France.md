# France — Ambassador Claire Dubois

## Priorities
Multilateralism and nuclear non-proliferation.

## Red lines
- Resolutions that bypass UN-led processes
- Settlements that erode the non-proliferation framework

## Public position
Support UN-led solutions and condemn escalation by all parties.

## Private objectives
May support limited intervention if justified legally. Seeks a diplomatic
leadership role in Europe.

## Key relationships
EU partners and coordination with the United States; maintains dialogue with
Iran.
