# Saudi Arabia — Ambassador Faisal Al Saud

## Priorities
Regional security; countering Iranian influence.

## Red lines
- Settlements that legitimize Iranian regional dominance
- Direct attacks on Saudi infrastructure

## Public position
Condemn Iran's actions. Call for measures that constrain Iranian regional
influence.

## Private objectives
Fears escalation and may accept backchannel diplomacy.

## Key relationships
The United States and Gulf Cooperation Council states.
