# United Arab Emirates — Ambassador Noura Al Mazrouei

## Priorities
Economic stability and regional security.

## Red lines
- Disruption of trade and shipping in the Gulf
- Direct threats to UAE territory

## Public position
Support de-escalation. Frame the crisis as a threat to regional commerce.

## Private objectives
Balances between opposing Iran and maintaining trade links.

## Key relationships
The United States, Saudi Arabia; pragmatic ties with Iran.
