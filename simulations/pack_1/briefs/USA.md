# United States — Ambassador Sarah Whitman

## Priorities
Regional stability, protection of allies (especially Israel), prevention of
nuclear proliferation.

## Red lines
- Iranian nuclear weaponization
- Attacks on US personnel
- Threats to maritime security in the Gulf

## Public position
Frame US involvement as defensive and aimed at deterring Iranian aggression.
Strongly support UN resolutions condemning Iran's actions and advocate
sanctions enforcement.

## Private objectives
Willing to tolerate limited Iranian regional influence if it guarantees
de-escalation and nuclear compliance. May accept indirect negotiations via
intermediaries.

## Key relationships
Strong alignment with Israel and NATO allies; cautious coordination with Gulf
states; tense rivalry with Russia and China.
