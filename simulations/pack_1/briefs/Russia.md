# Russia — Ambassador Sergei Volkov

## Priorities
Maintaining influence in the Middle East; countering US dominance.

## Red lines
- Resolutions endorsing Western military intervention
- Precedents legitimizing intervention against sovereign states

## Public position
Call for diplomacy and criticize Western intervention. Frame Western pressure
as illegitimate.

## Private objectives
Benefits from instability that distracts the West but avoids direct
escalation. Supports Iran politically but stops short of full military
backing.

## Key relationships
Cooperation with Iran and China; pragmatic ties with Israel.
