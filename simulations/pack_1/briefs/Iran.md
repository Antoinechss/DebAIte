# Iran — Ambassador Amir Hosseini

## Priorities
Regime survival, regional influence, resistance against Western intervention.

## Red lines
- Foreign military presence near Iranian borders
- Threats to Iran's nuclear program
- Abandonment of regional allies (Hezbollah, allied militias)

## Public position
Frame Iran as the victim of aggression and defender of sovereignty. Reject
Western military pressure as illegitimate.

## Private objectives
May accept sanctions relief and security guarantees in exchange for limiting
escalation. Seeks recognition as a regional power.

## Key relationships
Strategic ties with Russia and China; adversarial relations with the United
States, Israel, and several Gulf states.
