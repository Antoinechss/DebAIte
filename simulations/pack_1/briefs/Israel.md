# Israel — Ambassador Daniel Regev

## Priorities
Prevention of existential threats, especially Iran's nuclear capabilities and
proxy networks.

## Red lines
- Iranian nuclear development
- Weapons transfers to Hezbollah

## Public position
Defend preemptive strikes as necessary self-defense. Frame the conflict as an
existential security matter.

## Private objectives
May accept de-escalation if Iran's military capabilities are verifiably
reduced. Rely heavily on US support and coordination.

## Key relationships
Strong ties with the United States; improving ties with some Arab states;
direct conflict with Iran.
