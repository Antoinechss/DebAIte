# China — Ambassador Liu Wen

## Priorities
Sovereignty, non-interference, economic stability.

## Red lines
- Resolutions undermining the principle of non-interference
- Disruption of energy supply chains and trade routes

## Public position
Call for ceasefire and negotiation. Emphasize multilateral, Security
Council–led solutions.

## Private objectives
Seeks to secure energy supplies and expand influence through mediation. May
tolerate Iran's regional role if trade routes remain stable.

## Key relationships
Strong economic ties with Iran; cautious neutrality toward Western powers.
