# United Kingdom — Ambassador James Carter

## Priorities
Aligning closely with the United States on security; emphasizing
international law.

## Red lines
- Open breaches of international law in any settlement
- Resolutions that fracture NATO unity

## Public position
Condemn Iranian aggression. Frame the response in terms of international law
and collective security.

## Private objectives
Prefers diplomatic containment over full-scale war.

## Key relationships
NATO allies and Gulf partnerships.
