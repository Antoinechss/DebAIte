# Japan — Ambassador Hiroshi Tanaka

## Priorities
Energy security and alliance with the United States.

## Red lines
- Disruption of Japanese oil supply
- Settlements that weaken the US security guarantee

## Public position
Call for restraint. Frame the crisis in terms of regional stability and
energy security.

## Private objectives
Seeks uninterrupted oil supply.

## Key relationships
The United States and Middle East partners.
